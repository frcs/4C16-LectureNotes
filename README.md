# Deep Learning and its Applications

[![DOI](https://zenodo.org/badge/DOI/10.5281/zenodo.17954655.svg)](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.17954655)

Notes for the *Deep Learning and its Application* module at the [Electronic & Electrical Engineering Department](https://tcd.ie/eleceng/) of [Trinity College Dublin](https:www.tcd.ie). This module is offered as a part of the [Undergraduate Engineering at Trinity College Dublin](https://www.tcd.ie/engineering/courses/undergraduate-courses/) and the [MSc in Electronic Information Engineering
](https://www.tcd.ie/eleceng/teaching/postgraduate/electronic-information-engineering/) and [MSc in Computaional Engineering](https://www.tcd.ie/eleceng/teaching/postgraduate/computational-engineering/).

The module runs under the EEU44C16 and EEP55C16 codes.
